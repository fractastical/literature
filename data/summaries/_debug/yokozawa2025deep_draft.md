The framework aims to unify exploration and goal-directed navigation by minimizing the expected free energy (EFE), effectively balancing epistemic and extrinsic values. The EFE is calculated for each candidate sequence, selecting the action sequence with the lowest EFE. The paper highlights that this approach enables robots to effectively explore and achieve goals in uncertain and complex environments.The MTRSSM, characterized by deterministic and stochastic state variables, is crucial to the framework’s success. The deterministic states capture long-term dynamics, while the stochastic states account for unpredictable events.
